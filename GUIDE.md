# NMS10 — How to Use

NMS10 (nms10.online) is the community hub for the **No Man's Sky 10th Anniversary** celebration —
a global, cross-platform in-game gathering on **August 9, 2026 at 18:00 UTC**. It's a community
project, not affiliated with Hello Games.

## Getting Started

1. **No account needed** — the whole site is open. Just visit nms10.online
2. Optionally **set your Traveler identity** (click the badge in the header) — it pre-fills
   submission forms and personalizes your reward card
3. The header shows a **live countdown** to the gathering and the **current Steam player count**

---

## The Expedition — `/`

The home page hosts the **Expedition for the Dreamers**, a symbolic 8-milestone journey for the
anniversary.

### Track Your Milestones
- **Click any milestone card** to mark it complete (click again to unmark)
- It's an **honor system** — complete them at your own pace, no proof required
- Progress is saved **in your browser only**, and the progress bar tracks your X / 8 count

### The Eight Milestones

| # | Milestone | What to do |
|---|-----------|------------|
| 1 | Answer the Call | Be in-game on Aug 9, 2026 at 18:00 UTC |
| 2 | Echoes Across the Stars | Use **#NMS10** and spread the message |
| 3 | Honour the Symbol | Discover, build, use, or share the anniversary logo |
| 4 | A Universe Remembered | Share something inspired by your NMS journey |
| 5 | Rendezvous with Travellers | Take part in a community event or gathering |
| 6 | Constellations of Community | Help showcase an NMS community |
| 7 | A Tribute Among the Stars | Create or share an anniversary tribute |
| 8 | Voices of the Dreamers | Watch, join, or support anniversary content |

### Claim the Dreaming Traveller Card
- Complete **all 8 milestones** to unlock the reward section
- Click **Claim your card** — if you haven't set an identity yet, you'll be asked to first
- The card opens in DreamingFox's card generator, personalized with your name, race, platform,
  and sigil. It's a symbolic badge of participation, not an official reward

## Your Traveler Identity

- Click **Set your Traveler identity** in the header (or your name pill, once set) to open the form
- Fields: **Name** (required), **Race**, **Platform**, **Sigil** (30+ community emblems), and
  **Affiliation** (e.g. your civilization)
- It's stored **locally on your device only** — nothing is sent to a server
- It pre-fills the base and meetup submission forms and personalizes your Dreaming Traveller Card

## Civs & Bases — `/civs`

A showcase of communities and player builds, in two tabs.

### Communities
- The **Communities** tab lists approved NMS communities with language, description, and a
  **Visit** link
- Click **+ Submit a community** to add yours — name, language, description, and link

### Bases
- The **Bases** tab shows player builds as cards; click one to open its detail page
- **Filter by platform** (PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch) and **by tags** (megabase, settlement,
  outpost, underwater, themed, floating, monument) — tags combine, so each extra tag narrows
  the results

### Base Detail Pages — `/civs/bases/<id>`
- Hero shot, builder profile, full description, and photo gallery
- **Location card** — galaxy, region, platform
- **Portal address** — punch it in at any portal to visit the build yourself
- Stats: submission date, stars, and visits

### Submit a Base
- Click **+ Submit a base** on the Bases tab
- Required: **title** and **builder name** (pre-filled from your identity)
- Optional: affiliation, platform, galaxy, region, portal address, tags, description,
  builder's notes, plus an email or Discord ID so the team can follow up

## Meetups — `/meetups`

Find or organize **in-person gatherings** of Travelers worldwide.

- Browse the **world map** — every gold pin is a meetup; click a pin or a list card to fly to it
- **Filter by region**: Worldwide, Europe, North America, Asia-Pacific, South America
- Each card shows the date/time, title, location, and description

### Submit a Meetup
- Click **+ Submit a meetup**
- **Click the mini-map** in the form to drop a pin — it fills in latitude/longitude for you
- Add a title, region, location name, start time, description, organizer name, and a contact URL

---

## Socials — `/socials`

The **#NMS10 Across the Stars** wall — community posts about the anniversary in one feed.

- **Filter by source**: Twitter/X, Bluesky, YouTube, Reddit, TikTok, Discord
- Click any post card to **open the original** on its platform
- The feed is curated/aggregated by the team; automated pulls fill it in as they come online

## FAQ & Downloads — `/faq`

- **FAQ accordion** — the what/when/why of the celebration, the Expedition, the Dreaming
  Traveller Card, the anniversary logo, and the Hello Games disclaimer
- **Downloads & Assets** — community-made anniversary assets. Greyed-out tiles aren't ready
  yet; live ones (like the **DreamingFox Card Generator**) open in a new tab

## The Discord Bot

Everything you can submit on the site, you can also submit from Discord (in servers running
the NMS10 bot):

| Command | Does |
|---------|------|
| `/submit-base` | Submit a base for the showcase |
| `/submit-community` | Submit a community |
| `/submit-meetup` | Submit an IRL meetup |
| `/submit-social` | Submit a social media link about NMS10 |
| `/nms10-status` | Countdown + live stats for the anniversary |

---

## How Submissions Work

- Every submission (base, community, meetup, social link) goes into a **moderation queue** —
  it appears on the site once the team approves it
- You'll get a confirmation with a submission ID right away; approval isn't instant
- Submissions are **rate-limited per IP** — if you hit the limit, wait a bit or use the
  Discord bot's `/submit-*` commands instead
